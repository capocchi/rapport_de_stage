How it works

Templates jump start your apps

Honeycode’s fully functioning templates offer you a head start for your customized app. Check out our purpose-built templates to get started today.

Customize screens

You decide what shows on each app screen. Use your current processes to guide what your team will do in the app, then easily craft the screens to increase productivity.

Personalize for each team member

Choose what data each person can see and keep private data on a need-to-know basis. For example, an app that helps your team track tasks can show each person only the tasks assigned to them.

Automate workflows

Create forms, build approval processes, process data calculations, and customize almost any workflow you want.

Notify team members instantly

Keep your team in the loop about important updates. Automatically send emails and mobile notifications whenever key data changes.

Share with your team

When you're ready, invite anyone to use your app. They'll get an invite to join your team so you can work together.

Access anywhere, update anytime

Access your app from a browser or Honeycode's Android & iOS apps. Make changes to your app at any time and the updates happen instantly.

Import your existing spreadsheets

You likely have processes in place using spreadsheets. Upload those to Honeycode to power your new apps with that existing data.
